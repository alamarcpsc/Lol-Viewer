LolViewer
by Adam La Mar, Michael Bilsborough, and Matt Bilsborough
reads in data from 4Matt.csv which is data on your last 10 ranked games.

Main View:
Graphs number of objectives taken vs number of games played for both you and the enemy team.
Click on the Towers, Dragons, or Barons button to switch which objective you wanna see.
Each game number is a button which changes the data of the two sub views below.

Left Sub View:
Graphs the players Gold Per Minute, XP Per Minute, or CS Per Minute vs a timer interval. Use the buttons on the left to chose which per min data you want to see.

Right Sub View:
Graphs the players stats compared to that of what good stats should be. You cab click the button for each stat to choose if you want it to appear on the viewer.
